Rules and Regulations

Basketball

  • The Matches will be conducted in Knockout basis (starts at 22-feb-2019)
  • from 9Am.
  • Each Team must have 12 members only.
  • The official rules will be followed as per the rule book.
  • Only 1 team from a college is allowed.
  • Participants are required to produce their Id-card and permission letter
  • Including the bonafide certificate from the college to participate in the sports
    • fest.
  • Players must attend with their respective gaming uniform.
  • Eligibility is for every course that are available in that Engineering  college.
  • The tournament committee has the right to change the time, date or any other matter concerned with the tournament.
  • Teams must be present 30-45 minutes before the tournament commences.
  • Teams showing up after the tournament commences would not be allowed to play.
  • The registration fee is Rs.1000/- for men and Rs.700/- for women, after the registration the participants will get food coupons.

Cricket

  • The Matches will be conducted in Knockout basis.
  • The matches are conducted up to four days (starts from 20-feb-2019)
  • Each Team must have 16 members only.
  • Only 1 team from a college is allowed.
  • Participants are required to produce their Id-card and permission letter Including the bonafide certificate from the college to participate in the sports fest.
  • Players must attend with their respective gaming uniform (white jersey)
  • Eligibility is for every course that are available in that Engineering  college.
  • The tournament committee has the right to change the time, date or any other matter concerned with the tournament.
  • Each match in the first round will be of 12 overs. Each match will take about two hours.
  • Players should bring their own required equipment. Only the ball will be provided.
  • Semifinals will be of fifteen overs. Each match will take about two and half hours.
  • Teams must be present 30-45 minutes before the tournament commences.
  • Teams showing up after the tournament commences would not be allowed to play.
  • The registration fee is Rs.1500/- after the registration the participants will get food coupons.

Chess

  • 1-2 days (According to the number of teams participating)
  • League / Knockouts (According to the number of teams participating) starts at (22-feb-19) from 9 am.
  • Each Team must have 6 members only.
  • Only 1 team from a college is allowed.
  • Participants are required to produce their Id-card and permission letter Including the bonafide certificate from the college to participate in the sports fest.
  • Players must attend with their respective gaming uniform.
  • Eligibility is for every course that are available in that Engineering college.
  • The tournament committee has the right to change the time, date or any other matter concerned with the tournament.
  • A team can have both male and female players.
  • Players must bring two sets of their own Chess boards and clocks.

  • Teams must be present 30-45 minutes before the tournament commences.
  • Teams showing up after the tournament commences would not be allowed to play.
  • The registration fee is Rs.700/- after the registration the participants will get food coupons.
